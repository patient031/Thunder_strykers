About Us

The Thunder Strykers were founded in 2023 to bring people together through soccer. Competing in local Mexican tournaments, we have quickly grown into a symbol of unity and teamwork, proudly finishing second in our recent tournament. What began as a small group of passionate players has evolved into a diverse team, representing various backgrounds, cultures, and ideas.

The team was formed to provide a platform where community members could connect, share their love for the sport, and inspire one another to grow both on and off the field. The Thunder Strykers have built a reputation for their sportsmanship, skill, and dedication to the game through hard work, discipline, and unwavering determination.

In our short time, we've fostered a competitive spirit and established friendships and bonds that extend beyond the field. Whether it's the roar of the crowd during matches or the quiet moments of teamwork during practice, Thunder Strykers have become more than just a soccer team — we are a family united by our love for the game and our community.

Looking forward, we aim to continue competing at higher levels, growing our team, and inspiring future generations to pursue their passion for soccer while promoting unity, respect, and inclusivity within the community.
